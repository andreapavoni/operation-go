"Hey Jason! I have some great news for you. You're going into the field," yells your boss Rand.

This is the moment you've been dreading since you joined the agency. You're perfectly fine hunting down the notorious cyber criminal Epoch from your dual monitors at the agency.

"Intelligence is reporting that Epoch is on a remote island in the Pacific. You're on a plane at 0600, with a briefing on the flight. Don't let us down."

"Thank you sir," you reply drearily.

"Don't worry," Rand replies, "Operation Go should be pretty routine."

Nothing at this agency is routine you think as you pack up your field laptop and head to the airport. When you arrive at the plane, the officer on duty looks confused.

"Where's your gun Agent Marshall?" he barks.

As you look around, everyone else on the mission is in full tactical gear.

"There must be some mistake," you reply insistently. "Let me take a look..."
